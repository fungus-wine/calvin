Welcome to the Calvin project. Calvin is an autonomous, two wheeled self balancing robot. His purpose is yet to be determined, but he'll be able to roam about and talk to people withtout human intevention - eventually. It's going to be a long project. I only kind of know what I'm doing, so don't take anything written here as gospel.

I've completed a fair ammount of the design, but there is still much more unfinished than finished.

So far, I have a good start on the mechcanical design, the electrical components have been selected, and I've started working out the backbone code - the communication architecture that allows the components to communicate.
